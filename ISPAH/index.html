IPSAH Advantage

Work whenever, Wherever. Run your business via online pop up shops, social media, messaging, in-person events Flexible commitment – you decide your own schedule & hours. Set your personal goals & explore new passions along the way !

The IPSAH business model

All you need to do is place an order with us for the amount of product inventory you want and we will ship it you. Personal Sales : Receive a 25% to 33% commission on items that you sell. Team Sales : Receive commission based on items your team sells.

Story

Journey So Far

  • July 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    IPSAH was conceived as a place where smart, creative and confident people could play a significant role in creating an innovative new business model where they were at the center. IPSAH is both a community and opportunity that combines elements of direct sales, retail and eCommerce with the sole purpose of providing a more meaningful experience to the modern Indian. Our mission is to empower the next generation of Indian entrepreneurs through social retail.

  • November 2015

    A Community is Born

    IPSAH is an opportunity for young, ambitious individuals who aim and believe in being ones own boss. Today, our youth has a great drive to achieve something on their own, to be independent as well as create a substantial source of income. Many are moving away from home at an early age to pursue their education and work to save up for their future. With IPSAH we hope our youth are successful in making money by confidently running their own business and build their resume with an even wider set of skills as well as connect to a community of like-minded individuals.

  • 2016 & Beyond

    Transition to Full Service

    IPSAH is powered by Indian women, the heart and soul of our platform, and the true centre of our company. Every piece purchased through this platform helps her reach a personal goal, whether it’s paying off a college bill, supporting the needs of her family, or starting out on a new career path. Our mission is to help tackle the financial needs of the modern woman - to provide the training and tools to accomplish her goals, whatever they may be.
